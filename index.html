IPSN Overview

Although there are about 70 Plastic surgery societies in the world , but IPSN Society is totally different and unique.

6 years Ago, IPSN was born on Facebook as an innovative idea from 2 surgeons one from Italy and one from Libya. Then they were merged into one Community which is IPSN . The main aim was to connect all the plastic surgeons from all over the world together , let them share their experiences and help the surgeons who were in need.
